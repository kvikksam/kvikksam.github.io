How to Use the Site

The Anatomical Collection website is a tool for a better guidance and understanding of the specimens in the collection.

​When viewing the collection, you will notice there being QR-codes attached to every specimen label.​

​ Scan these QR-codes to enter the website and reveal interesting information both about the specific specimen, for example if an organ has a disease or a unique history, and about the specimen group more generally.

​ If your interest is peaked, even more information about dissection history and specimen conservation is easily available though the website.
